What kind animals are the bird?

Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates (vertebrates have backbones) and are the only animals with feathers. Although all birds have wings, a few species can't fly.

What do birds do?

As members of ecosystems, birds play many roles, including as predators, pollinators, scavengers, seed dispersers, seed predators, and ecosystem engineers.

How many species of birds have?

The number of bird species is about twice the number of mammal species in the world. Scientists estimate that there are between 9,000 and 11,000 species of birds in the world today.
